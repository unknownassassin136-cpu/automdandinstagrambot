# AJR Digital Hub — Business Context

## About Us
AJR Digital Hub is a 3D printing services and 3D printer sales company based in India. We specialize in custom 3D prints, rapid prototyping, and selling high-quality 3D printers for hobbyists and professionals.

## Products & Services
- **Custom 3D Printing**: We print your designs using PLA, ABS, and PETG materials.
- **3D Printer Sales**: We sell 3D printers from leading brands.
- **Rapid Prototyping**: Fast turnaround for product prototypes.
- **Bulk Orders**: Discounted rates for large quantity orders.
- **Design Services**: We can help convert your ideas into printable 3D models.

## Materials Available
- PLA (Polylactic Acid) — Most popular, eco-friendly
- ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) — Strong, heat-resistant
- PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) — Durable, flexible

## Pricing

- ABS and PETG may have slightly different pricing.
- For exact quotes, customers should send their STL file.
- 3D printer prices vary by model — contact us for current pricing.
